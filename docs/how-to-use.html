Board Analytics

Powerful analytics and insights for your monday.com boards

1 Getting Started

Board Analytics is a Board View that provides real-time analytics and visualizations for your monday.com boards. After installation, add it to any board:

  1. Open your board
  2. Click the + Add View button
  3. Search for "Board Analytics"
  4. Click to add it to your board

The app will automatically load your board data and display analytics.

3 Tab Views

Board Analytics organizes insights into four tabs:

Overview
Status donut chart + Group bar chart
Distribution
Detailed status and group breakdowns
Timeline
Items created over last 30 days
Issues
Overdue and stale items tables

Overview Tab

The Overview tab shows two main visualizations:

  • Status Distribution (Donut Chart): Shows how items are distributed across different status values
  • Items by Group (Bar Chart): Shows the number of items in each group

Distribution Tab

Provides detailed bar charts showing the full breakdown of statuses and groups with exact counts and percentages.

Timeline Tab

Displays a line chart showing how many items were created each day over the last 30 days. Useful for tracking activity trends and workload patterns.

Issues Tab

Shows two sortable tables:

  • Overdue Items: Items past their due date (requires date column selection)
  • Stale Items: Items not updated in the longest time

4 Column Configuration

Board Analytics automatically detects your columns, but you can customize:

Status Column

Select which status column to use for the status distribution charts. The app will auto-detect columns of type "Status" or "Color".

Date Column

Select a date column to enable overdue item detection. The app will compare item dates against today to identify overdue tasks.

Tip
If you have multiple status or date columns, use the dropdowns to switch between them and see different perspectives on your data.

5 Understanding the Charts

Donut Chart (Status Distribution)

  • Shows the proportion of items in each status
  • Hover over segments to see exact counts and percentages
  • Legend shows the top 6 statuses
  • Center displays total item count

Bar Chart

  • Horizontal bars show relative quantities
  • Numbers on the right show exact counts
  • Hover for percentage of total
  • Colors match your monday.com status/group colors

Line Chart (Timeline)

  • X-axis shows dates (last 30 days)
  • Y-axis shows number of items created
  • Dots represent data points for each day
  • Shows total and average items per day

Data Tables

  • Click column headers to sort
  • Shows top 10 items by default
  • Color-coded severity indicators

6 Dark Mode

Board Analytics automatically adapts to your monday.com theme settings:

  • Light mode: Clean white backgrounds
  • Dark mode: Dark backgrounds with adjusted colors
  • Charts and metrics adjust automatically

No configuration needed - it follows your monday.com account theme preference.

  • Track overdue items for deadline management

Team Leads

  • See work distribution across groups
  • Identify bottlenecks in status flow
  • Monitor team activity with timeline

Executives

  • Quick board health overview
  • Status distribution for reporting
  • Identify areas needing attention

8 Refreshing Data

Board Analytics loads data when you open the view. To refresh:

  • Click the Refresh button in the top-right corner
  • Or reload the board view

Data is fetched in real-time from your board, so analytics always reflect the current state.
