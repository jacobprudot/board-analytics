Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Board Analytics ("we", "our", or "the App") is a monday.com marketplace application that provides analytics and insights for your boards. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Access

Board Analytics accesses the following data through the monday.com API:

  • Board Data: Item names, creation dates, update dates, and column values
  • Column Configuration: Column types and settings for proper analytics display
  • Group Information: Group names and colors for distribution analysis

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of this data to external servers. All data processing occurs entirely within your browser and the monday.com platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data accessed is used solely to:

  • Calculate and display analytics metrics (item counts, activity trends)
  • Generate visualizations (charts, graphs, tables)
  • Identify overdue and stale items based on dates
  • Show status and group distributions

4. Data Storage

Board Analytics does NOT store any of your data. The app:

  • Does not have external databases
  • Does not use cookies for your board data
  • Does not transmit data to third-party services
  • Processes all data in real-time through monday.com's secure API
  • Does not persist any analytics results

5. Data Security

Your data security is ensured through:

  • monday.com's OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • monday.com's secure API infrastructure
  • Read-only access (we cannot modify your data)
  • No external data transmission or storage

6. Third-Party Services

Board Analytics does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services. The app operates exclusively within the monday.com ecosystem.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Uninstall the app at any time from your monday.com account
  • Revoke API permissions through monday.com's app management
  • Contact us for any privacy-related questions

8. Children's Privacy

Board Analytics is a business productivity tool and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the monday.com marketplace listing.
